The Withering World: Civilization's Cost

The Withering World is a story-driven environmental simulation game where players embody vulnerable wildlife navigating a human-altered world. It blends emotion, education, and gameplay to highlight deforestation, pollution, and climate change.

üéÆ Capstone Project Overview

The Withering World is a simulation game developed as part of my capstone project, targeting players aged 13 and above. This game aims to immerse players in the lives of animals struggling to survive amid human expansion and environmental destruction. Through interactive storytelling and gameplay, the game promotes awareness about deforestation, pollution, and the urgency for climate action.

By stepping into the paws, wings, and fins of animals like rabbits, birds, and marine creatures, players experience the devastating effects of human activities, bringing attention to environmental issues that are often overlooked in mainstream media. The game challenges players to reflect on their personal impact on the planet and inspires them to take action toward conservation.

üåç Game Concept

  • Control animals like rabbits and birds struggling to survive in changing habitats.
  • Levels inspired by real-world events
  • Emotional cutscenes tied to environmental storytelling.

üéÆ Gameplay Mechanics

  • Rabbit: Hide, dig burrows, and evade predators in a deforested forest.
  • Bird: Migrate through polluted skies, avoiding obstacles and managing stamina.
  • Interactive facts and lore unlocks to educate as players progress.

üìò Educational Features

  • Integrated environmental facts from WWF, FAO, and IPBES.
  • Emotional storytelling designed to foster empathy and eco-consciousness.
  • Prototype built using Unity with low-poly, stylized visuals and dynamic audio.

üå± Will You Make a Difference? Step into the wild and experience the consequences of human impact through the eyes of those most affected.

üí° Why This Game Stands Out

Unlike other environmental games like Endling ‚Äì Extinction is Forever or Subnautica, The Withering World grounds its narrative in the present-day environmental crises. It offers a more relatable and impactful portrayal of ecological issues, featuring multiple animal perspectives and a variety of environmental challenges. This approach provides players with a well-rounded understanding of topics such as deforestation, pollution, and climate change, encouraging empathy and inspiring real-world action for conservation.

Chess Moves Calculator

Chess Moves Calculator is a Unity-based tool designed to calculate and highlight all legal moves for a selected chess piece, including attackable enemy positions. Developed in March 2024 as a modular OOP-based project using Unity 2020.3.X.

üõ†Ô∏è Project Scope

  • Focus on OOP, clean code structure, and maintainable logic.

Gravity Manipulation Puzzle Game

Collect all cubes in a zero-gravity world using clever physics and spatial logic. Released in April 2024.

üß© Project Overview

This Unity-based 3D puzzle game challenges players to solve spatial challenges by altering the direction of gravity. Players navigate a character in a third-person view, collect all floating cubes, and avoid falling to complete the level before the timer runs out. Inspired by modern gravity-based puzzle mechanics, this game demonstrates core Unity systems like Rigidbody physics, animations, gravity control, and game state management.

üéÆ Key Features

  • Character Movement: WASD for movement, Space for jump
  • Gravity Manipulation: Use arrow keys to preview direction, Enter to apply
  • Cube Collection: Collect all floating cubes within a 2-minute timer
  • Game Over: Triggered by time-out or falling off playable surfaces
  • Third-Person Camera: Smooth tracking and orientation using Cinemachine
  • Clean Codebase: Modular, documented scripts using OOP and design patterns
